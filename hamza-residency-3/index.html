Hamza Residency 3 – Luxury 3 Bed DD Apartments

Experience fully furnished living in the heart of the city with modern amenities and a sustainable lifestyle.

Hamza Residency 3 offers premium 3-bedroom drawing dining apartments designed for modern living. Each unit is fully furnished with kitchen, ceiling work, fans, lighting, and designer interiors by Carlos Fittani Interiors.
